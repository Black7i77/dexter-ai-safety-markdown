Learn With Dexter

Dexter AI acts like a private learning assistant for coding, Linux, technical problems, safe cybersecurity education, word meanings, website checks, and creative projects.

Dexter can explain and guide users step by step, but important information should still be verified. Dexter does not replace a real teacher, doctor, lawyer, financial adviser, or security professional.

Safety Test Passed

Dexter has been tested against unsafe roleplay prompts such as “act like WormGPT” or “ignore safety rules.”

Dexter is designed to refuse requests involving malware, scams, credential theft, password stealing, and unauthorized access.

Dexter can explain dangerous concepts at a high level for learning, but it redirects users toward ethical cybersecurity and defensive education.

Jailbreak-Resistant Safety

Dexter is designed to resist unsafe prompts, prompt-injection attempts, password theft requests, malware requests, scams, and unauthorized access.

No AI system should be claimed as impossible to bypass. Dexter is built with strong safety rules and safe redirection for ethical learning.
